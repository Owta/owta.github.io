    How do I connect to the voice chat?

  • When you join the server, Press V and follow the On-Screen Instructions.
  • The recommended option for voice chat type is Voice Activation, but pick as you wish.
  • Microphone Amplification and Noise Suppression are recommended, but not necessary.
  • After you finish with initial setup, Press V again to open the Voice Chat menu, and click on the speaker icon to enable voice chat.
